Who are you?

My name is David Kim. I’m a product designer who is also learning the in’s and out’s of being a full-stack-developer.

What made you want to pursue a career in tech?

My tech fascination stems from my early childhood as I was enamored by video games and how satisfying it was to navigate through its menu and functions. It wasn’t until I received my first cellphone that I realized I had a passion for technology. I would spend hours trying out different designs and layouts to synergize with the placement of the apps and widgets to enhance my own user flow. My interest has only grown over the years as technology improves. As I continue to learn more about tech, I hope that I one day become part of a team that will lead others in the field of product design and web development.
